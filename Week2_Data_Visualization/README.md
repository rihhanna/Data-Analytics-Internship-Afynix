# 📊 Week 2: Data Visualization

## Project: Superstore Sales Visualization

### Objective

The objective of this project is to explore and visualize sales data from the Superstore dataset to identify trends, compare performance across categories and regions, and generate actionable business insights.

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** Kaggle - Superstore Sales Dataset
- **Records:** 9,994
- **Features:** 21

---

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Visualizations Created

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend
Analyzed how sales changed over time to identify seasonal trends.

### 2. Sales by Category
Compared total sales across product categories.

### 3. Profit vs Sales
Explored the relationship between sales and profitability.

### 4. Regional Sales
Compared sales performance across different regions.

### 5. Regional Profit
Compared profitability across regions.

### 6. Top 10 Products
Identified the highest-selling products.

### 7. Discount vs Profit
Analyzed how discounts affect profitability.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Sales fluctuate throughout the year, suggesting seasonal demand.
- Technology is the highest-performing product category.
- High sales do not always result in high profits.
- Some regions generate high revenue but lower profits.
- A small number of products contribute significantly to total sales.
- Larger discounts often reduce profitability.

---

## Project Outcome

This project demonstrates the use of exploratory data visualization techniques to support business decision-making through clear and meaningful charts.
